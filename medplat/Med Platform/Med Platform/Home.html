Our Business

Hercules Group is focused on offering high quality medical diagnostic service to our customers. Each and every individual within Hercules Group knows that only by running a highly efficient group practice, building partnerships with key customers, meeting our customers needs and producing advanced medical equipment for carrying out medical tests, will it be able to improve its aim of providing high quality medical care. Our diagnostic centres are equipped to perform a number of diagnostic tests. The location of our centres near MTR Stations makes them easy to find and reach. Professionalism and quality lie at the heart of the Hercules Group. In order to accomplish our commitments, all medical diagnostic procedures are handled by experienced specialists and performed with world-class equipment. Besides, our professional customer service team is always ready to offer its customers services beyond their expectations.

Our Aim

Since its establishment in 2004, Hercules Medical Diagnostic and Laboratory Group has been committed to serving the public through comprehensive medical diagnostic services of superior standard and quality. Throughout these years, we contributed to the community by providing professional and reliable health check services. We realize that the escalating healthcare costs have created a heavy burden on the Government and the public. The expenditure of public health services constituted 14.4% of recurrent public expenditure in 2004-05. Solo-practice clinics or individual specialists, however, have been finding it difficult to maintain the balance between price and quality of service. We believe that delivering comprehensive and high quality medical diagnostic services in a reasonable price range through group practice is a path to achieve business excellence.
